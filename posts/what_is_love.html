What is love?

Sept 2025

What is love? Is it really the selfless unconditional act of giving?

They say love without demanding; be compassionate and spread kindness. It will definitely come back to you. But isn't that being selfish? Doing something good in hope that it will return to us somehow; hoping someone, someday will reciprocate the same feeling with which you are currently overflowing, believing someone will love you with the same intensity and passion with which you love the world - isn't it foolish?

Is love craving for love, or is it believing that someone will fill the void in you? Seeing someone happy makes you happy, taking part in someone's sorrow is something you naturally do... is this love even if the feeling is not mutual?

Why is it so difficult for someone to feel love, even though they say, they want to learn how to love from you?

Hopelessly romanticizing your day dreams; contemplating each and every moment again and again if I could have done things a better way to make someone's life better - is it going to take me far? Or is it going to take me far away from myself?

Am I hopelessly in love or am I hopeless in love?

They say don't search for love, you are love.
What am I?

Everything in this world comes with its counterpart. Power with responsibilities, fame with hatred, knowledge with despair, and love?
Love with sorrow, grief, pain, solitude and questions. With all these counterparts, will I truly be able to experience love? Or will it always remain as a never-ending illusion for me with all the darkness in the universe?

Will I be able to feel warmth again?

Is it wrong for me to ask for warmth? Is it wrong for me to question? Is it wrong for me to think in the first place? Am I wrong? If not, then what do I feel guilty for?

Life is full of unanswerable questions. But at least I am enjoying this; this very moment...

- Pratham
